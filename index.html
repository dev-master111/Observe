Observe

Bringing Computer Vision and AI to Aquaculture

The Problem

Feeding and health observation of fish stock in farms can account for almost 50% of production costs in Aquaculure. Ensuring each and every fish is optimally fed, healthy and environmentally safe is a massive costs to farmers. In the UK this entire process is heavily regulated by governmental bodies such as SEPA.

Combining Computer vision and Artificial Intelligence

Our powerful machine learning algorithms extract real-time analytics of the behavior and movement of aquaculture stock. Observe can combine the video stream data with existing sensory data platforms such as oxygen and temperature sensors. With this data, farmers can make data-driven decisions related to feeding, the health of the farm, and the environmental impact of the farm.
